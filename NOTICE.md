# Attribution

This repository is a clean, data-free research extension of
[Thought2Text](https://github.com/abhijitmishra/Thought2Text), originally
released by Abhijit Mishra under the MIT License.

The upstream pipeline builds on ChannelNet from:

> S. Palazzo, C. Spampinato, I. Kavasidis, D. Giordano, J. Schmidt, and
> M. Shah, "Decoding Brain Representations by Multimodal Learning of Neural
> Activity and Visual Features," IEEE TPAMI, 2020.

This extension adds the 16-to-128 channel adapter, low-density channel
selection workflow, edge export utilities, checkpoint compression, and
deployment-oriented benchmarks described in the README.
